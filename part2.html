How It Works

From resume to hire
in 4 simple steps

No training. No setup headaches. Just upload resumes and let AI do the heavy lifting.

Step 01

Upload Resumes

Step 02

AI Scans & Parses

Our AI engine extracts skills, experience, and qualifications from every resume in seconds — no manual reading needed.

Step 03

Candidates Ranked

Every applicant gets an AI match score against your job description. Top candidates rise to the top automatically.

Step 04

Hire the Best

Review shortlisted candidates, share profiles with your team, and make faster, smarter hiring decisions with confidence.
